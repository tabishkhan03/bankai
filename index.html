ABOUT US

Bankai Film is a new-age production house

Bankai Film is a new-age production house making its debut in filmmaking with Matsya Vedh. It is backed by a media and telecommunications conglomerate Bankai Group which has been in existence for more than 3 decades.

Bankai Films envisions producing cinema that has a global reach and audience. Starting with the web series Matsya Vedh, their vision is to expand the horizons in th entertainment industry and venture into making path-breaking feature films, web series, and more.
